# MultipleFigures
I invite you to journey across the world with me. 
Open your eyes and your mind and look at the world in a completely different way.
But before, I would like to make sure that you understand that I mean no disrespect to anyone or any entity. 
I also feel the need to warn you that after reading this book, you may never see things the same way ever again.

I invite you to zoom in and out of the images. This is not some kind of art project on my part. 
You don't have to accept right away what I am going to show you, I expect you to look for yourself and make up your own mind.

I could have added a lot more example, but I tried to provide enough material to show you that this is not an isolated event, but there may be an intention behind this phenomenon.
There is minimum text on purpose. I want you to be curious, look for yourself and discover the history and the beauty of world around us.

The lack of interpretation of the discovered shapes is intentional, as I do not want to impose my view on what you are looking at.

I think what I am presenting in this manuscript ties in very nicely with the Chase Hughes video [They Ancients Decoded Reality](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ADYdypHZb2A)

I genuinely believe that this book should be free. That is why I selected this format. This knowledge belong to everybody and not only a selected few.

And if this inspires you to give something, look around you, I am sure that there is someone that can use some help. 
Give around you, respect and forgive as we are all connected.

Finally, I am sure you already do, but please continue to honor and care for the Earth we call home.

Take care
